Steps to make Vegetable maggie

Ingridents prepration
  1. Take a packet of maggie and open it
  2. bring out the maggie and masala packet
  3. open the masala packet
  4. put maggie and masala aside in a plate
Preparing the Vegetables
  1. Take the onion, tomota and chillies
  2. Wash the Vegetables
    1. Chop onions
    2. chop tomatoes
    3. chop chillies
  3. Put all the chopped vegetables in a plate
Start with making the maggie
  1. Boil water in the bowl
  2. put the vegetables, maggie and masala in the boiling water
  3. cook everything for 10 minutes
